NiceTec Group

NiceTec Group is the pioneer IT training provider in Chhattisgarh and Madhya Pradesh). The Country was gearing up for a major change with a evolution during early 90’s, as Information Technology has just arrived in India to boost the Indian industrial market. So the NiceTec took this kind responsibility to make the entire people aware of the usefulness of IT in this life and career. The NiceTec formed a committed team to explore the awareness in the whole region. In the result over 150 training center have been opened so far across the country.

Established and Founded in the year 1992 under the Chairmanship of Mr. Ravindra Pratap Singh. In this long time lacs of students has been trained by NiceTec Group and his Franchisees. NiceTec Computer Edcuation Pvt. Ltd. is a part of NiceTec Group under franchisee center and other projects is executing. NiceTec Society for Electronics and Computer Technology (Regd. With the course of time NiceTec Group succeeded to open more than 150 franchise training centers so far. across the country.
